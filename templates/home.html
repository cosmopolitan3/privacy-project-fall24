Data Colonialism

Exploring the Global Exploitation of Data

Understanding how the control and extraction of data from marginalized communities around the world mirrors historical forms of colonialism.

What is Data Colonialism?

Data colonialism refers to the exploitation and control of data from historically oppressed and marginalized communities, akin to the exploitation of resources during the colonial era. In today's digital world, powerful entities extract data from individuals, often without their informed consent or adequate compensation, perpetuating cycles of inequality and harm.
